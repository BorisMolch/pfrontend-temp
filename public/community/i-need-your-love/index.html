I need your love

A simple jazz tune with added voice.

Jonathan Bell
Barcelona

Jonathan Bell (1982) is a composer researcher. He graduated from the National Conservatory of Music of Paris (CNSM, 4 prizes), and the Guildhall School of Music and Drama (Doctorate in Musical Composition). In 2014-16, he followed a two-years IRCAM residency. His music and scientific publications are played and presented in numerous computer music symposiums such as ICMC (New-York), NIME (Porto Allegre), TENOR (la Coruna, Montreal, Melbourne, Hamburg), SMC (Torino) and CMMR (Marseille).
